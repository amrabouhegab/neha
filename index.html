Welcome

Hello! I am Amr, based in Egypt

I am a creative Wedding, Lifestyle and Fashion photographer focusing on natural light. I aim to create a relaxed and comfortable, yet fun environment, so that I can deliver an organic collection of images that represents you! I like to be creative with my photography, yet produce timeless photos that you'll love forever! I'm a picture-taker because the process constantly reminds me to celebrate life and beauty, and the One who created it.
